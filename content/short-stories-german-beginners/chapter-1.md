# Die verrückte Currywurst
## The Crazy Currywurst

Eine Kurzgeschichte in 4 Kapiteln.

Daniel and his sister Julia travel from London to Cologne as exchange students. A wrong bus, a runaway trip to Frankfurt, and a mysterious old man in the back of a truck turn a simple dinner plan into an unexpected adventure.

Diese Geschichte hat 4 Kapitel. Viel Spaß beim Lesen!
