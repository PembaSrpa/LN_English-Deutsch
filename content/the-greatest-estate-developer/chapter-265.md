# Mermaids Love Jjimjilbangs (4)

---

You may run away from Hell, but the real Hell awaits you there. Lloyd wasn’t the type to believe those kinds of sayings. But that changed when he tried out another part-time job after becoming tired of doing grueling construction work under the hot sun.

That happened on the first day I was out for the mascot costume job!

It was a temp job at a newly opened fried chicken restaurant. Lloyd was asked to distribute ad flyers in a chicken mascot costume. It was during one of the hottest days of the summer when he got a taste of Hell.

I really thought I was going to die.

At first, Lloyd rejoiced. He was away from the typical harsh, grimy construction sites and trying out something else. But his joy and excitement vanished one minute into the job. Under the sun during the hottest days of the summer, he was tasked to go around the streets in a fluffy chicken costume and give out leaflets, all while putting on a charming act. That day, he learned that humans could sweat as much as he did then and the neighborhood kids darting at him in cackles and kicking him were devils. On top of everything, he also learned the cold, hard truth that the world loved nothing more than a handsome face.

Was it Yeongjin? Whew. The name is stuck in my head.

Yeongjin was his coworker at the job. Lloyd met him for the first time that day. But the name could not be erased from his head because of a notably awful incident that occurred on this particular date.

Yeongjin suggested we get a can of soda before we collapse to the ground from heat exhaustion.

Yeongjin said it’d be his treat. Having wanted to rest as well, Lloyd hurried to the convenience store and bought some sports drinks. They sat under a parasol in front of the store to hydrate themselves. When they both removed their costume head, chaos broke loose.

When Yeongjin threw off the costume head, it revealed his gorgeous face… And he brushed his sweat-soaked hair as he chugged his canned drink and wiped the sweat on his pale cheek… Then under his sharp jawline, his Adam’s apple bobbed… Which caused a few young female students passing by to scream in shock.

That day, the chicken restaurant raked in money. The first day of business was a slam dunk. What was more, Yeongjin went viral on social media as the “Hongdae Chicken Mascot Man.” The last time Lloyd heard, Yeongjin was scouted into some sort of talent agency.

“…”

Handsome guys were so disgustingly unfair. They had it so easy for everything. Lloyd shook his head and shoved the memory deep into his brain before looking up. The lessons Lloyd learned that day were being successfully applied here. As the shell crushed under the pressure of Lloyd’s hands and revealed Javier’s face, the latter’s gorgeousness allured everyone, who screamed in shock.

After all, it only took his face to get permission to enter this kingdom.

Javier was handsome. That was a sufficiently proven fact. Lloyd purposefully set up a scene with some slight nudging. The status quo was poles apart from the days when Lloyd asked the merpeople around about the jewel. Back then, Javier had his face cast down, never looking up even once due to embarrassment, and the merpeople were busy swimming off from Lloyd’s unending questions. As such, most of the merpeople didn’t get a good look at Javier’s face. However, that wasn’t the case today. His face was plainly revealed to every merperson gathered around them after Lloyd cracked the shell he was wearing.

The result? Victory. A couple of the merpeople drawn by Javier’s face became their first jjimjilbang customers. They entered in confusion at first, looking around, but all came out happy and relaxed. And then they started to spread the news about the facility to the others, how there was this place called a jjimjilbang, how it was the perfect place to relax your muscles and heal your joint pain. And all for free! It was safe even! The stories of the facility traveled surprisingly fast with great detail throughout the city. And six days after the jjimjilbang’s opening…

Clamor! Clamor! Everywhere Lloyd looked, the entire jjimjilbang was packed with merpeople. Some were walking as their silky hair danced sideways, and some were relaxing their defined muscles. Some were moving their fins happily, and some were sleeping. All of them were having a fantastic time in their own way. However, that wasn’t all.

“Come on! I was first in the line!”

“Liar, liar, pants on fire! I have been standing in line since last night!”

There were constant quarrels outside the jjimjilbang as Lloyd started to limit entry due to overcapacity after too many merpeople came rushing in at once. As such, the merpeople outside often cut in line and fought as they waited to get in.

This is nothing less than incredible.

The corners of his lips rose in contentment. His plan to mobilize the merpeople through the jjimjilbang was a stellar success. So, now…

Time to move on to the next stage.

Lloyd sensed that the time was now. Since he had given the merpeople the heavenly taste of the jjimjilbang, it was time to move on to his next step. That night, Lloyd set up a big sign at the entrance of the jjimjilbang.

***

The following morning rolled around.

“Oh my? What is this?” A granny mermaid rubbed her senile eyes in disbelief. She tilted her muscular neck and stared hard at the sign in front of the jjimjilbang entrance. And the words in it gutted her deeply.

[Jjimjilbang Promotional Event Is Now Over]

[From today, the jjimjilbang will no longer be free. For more information, please read below. Thank you.]

What is happening now?

The granny mermaid clenched her fists reflexively. No more free entrances? A sort of payment was required to use the jjimjilbang, perhaps? There was now a price to treat her entire body with warm water and forget about her aching joints?

What is the payment, then?

The granny mermaid had come here with the resolution to further enjoy the sauna rooms than the day before and treat herself the entire day until night came. As such, the granny mermaid was flustered, to say the least. In one part, she also felt nervous. Calming her nerves, she looked somewhere else.

Look below for more information?

She looked down and found a flat rock that held several rolls of refined seaweed. She picked one up and opened it to find tiny words written on it.

“Jjimjilbang Sign-up Form?” The wrinkles on her forehead deepened. Her eyes quickly moved.

[Seabed Cave Jjimjilbang Sign-up Form]

[This form serves the purpose of ensuring the best and smoothest experience possible for our customers. Please carefully read the terms and conditions before filling out the form.]

Terms and conditions?

The granny mermaid felt an inexplicable sense of anxiety creeping up inside her. Her eyes moved to the terms and conditions.

[Jjimjilbang Membership Terms and Conditions]

(1) Seabed Cave Jjimjilbang (“Jjimjilbang”) is strictly membership-based. Those who are not members will be restricted from entering and patronizing the facility.

(2) The maximum hours the members can patronize the facility is 12 hours every day.

(3) All members must pay a small fee to patronize the jjimjilbang. The small fee will not come in monetary or material form. It shall be in the form of labor in the areas designated by the owner of the jjimjilbang, Lloyd Frontera.

Shut up! Labor? I have to work for Lloyd Frontera and go wherever that land human wants me to! For what? To use the facility?

Fright filled her eyes. At the same time, a crippling sense of anger came over her.

Is this human a madman or what? What does he think about us merpeople?

She was upset. She wouldn’t feel this way if Lloyd had asked for precious shells or pearls. But instead, he was demanding the merpeople to work for him.

What an impertinent human!

She shook her head, affirming her perception of humans once again. Pesky and nosy. Environment destroyers. Her angry eyes quickly moved to continue and read the rest.

(4) The number of labor hours required to use the jjimjilbang will be half of an individual’s time spent inside the facility. (For example, if you stayed six hours, you should work three hours.)

(5) Members must labor within two days (48 hours) of using the jjimjilbang. If the members fail to comply within the time frame, they will face one of three restrictions in using the facility.

What? Restrictions in using the facility?

She clicked her tongue. The labor compensation part was already ludicrous enough, and now what? Members will be banned from using the jjimjilbang if they don’t pay up within 48 hours? Nothing was more tyrannical than this.

Fine. I’ll see what restrictions you have set forth.

A glint of righteous anger appeared in her eyes.

(6) Usage restrictions in the event of failure to pay.

① One default: Ban from the jjimjilbang for ten days.

② Two defaults: Ban from the jjimjilbang for 30 days.

③ Three defaults: Loss of membership and the right to sign up again. A permanent ban from the jjimjilbang.

(7) First-come, first-served signup event: The first 100 members to sign up will receive a complimentary 60 hours on us.

The granny mermaid was at a loss for words. So, in short, to sum up the terms and conditions…

I have to work to use the jjimjilbang, and I’ll get three warnings if I don’t. After three strikes, it’s a permanent ban.

She scoffed dryly. The more she thought about it, the more speechless she became. What kind of a human would do such a thing? Worm-like veins in her curved upper torso popped in anger.

“Like hell I would! What a filthy antic! I won’t use it then.”

Rip! The seaweed scroll paper was torn into shreds in her hands, and she turned away. Her pecs bulged out in anger. This reaction was similar to every person that came after her.

“Sure, I love relaxing in a hot sauna… But enough to work for a human?”

“Oh, come on! This clearly shows how he looks down on us merpeople!”

“I know, right? Even though one of them is drop-dead gorgeous and the sauna is very relaxing and comfortable, this is too much. He’s asking for our labor as payment, not pearls? What does he think of us? Does he see us as cheap labor for him to exploit?”

“Nope. I’ll just stop going to his sauna.”

“Me neither. We’ve been fine all along without this jjimjilbang.”

“Yes, indeed. It was never part of our lifestyle anyway. We can live without it just fine.”

“I think so too! Let’s just leave the jjimjilbang as a nice memory of the past!”

Some merpeople felt resentful when they read the signup form. Some were enraged. They enjoyed relaxing their muscles and joints with heat, but this was too much. It was a direct mockery to all merpeople. So, they scoffed, arguing that they’ve been living without the jjimjilbang just fine and that they’ll survive without it well enough. Every one of them agreed and left in a rage, resolving that they would never ever use the facility again. They coldly went away, and that day, not a single one of them signed up. As such, the jjimjilbang became silent for the first time in a while. The red clay room, which once bustled with merpeople, was now still. The seawater room and the tropical beach sand room that the merpeople loved for their familiar charm and exotic vibe, respectively, were only occupied by deep-sea plankton.

“It appears that we have failed.” Javier, unable to hold any longer, crinkled his brow.

“I don’t think so,” disagreed Lloyd, shrugging his shoulders. “Things will change in a few days.”

“How will it change?”

“Wait, and you will see.” Lloyd grinned.

Lloyd was confident. He was confident that even though the merpeople had initially turned away in frustration after reading the membership form, the situation would change in the next few days. So, he simply waited without a hint of doubt in his head. As time passed, the merpeople were still angry, and Lloyd was still confident. One day. Two days. Four days. Six days later. On that sixth day, at last, the merpeople realized. They were forced to admit it. They had to admit that they could not live without the jjimjilbang anymore.
