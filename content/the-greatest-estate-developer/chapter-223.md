# An All-out War (2)

---

Clank! Lloyd brought out a foldable shovel in the saddle, opened it up, and grabbed it. His heart started to pound.

Can I do it?

Lloyd quickly racked his head. He had to save Javier as soon as possible. The man could be on the brink of death right now. To that end, he couldn’t let his emotions rule him.

I must remain rational at times like this.

And calm. Lloyd’s gaze cooled down as he pulled himself together. He thought about the situation with great care but also objectively. He formulated a plan in his head. The Bone Dragon was blocking his way, but he needed to get to Javier. After a short deliberation, he quickly came to a decision.

I can do it.

There was no time to waste. He started moving right away.

“Ggoming!” Lloyd called.

“Ggoming!” replied Ggoming as he fluttered his wings. He surged high by pretending to take a left turn. The Bone Dragon fluttered its wings energetically, soaring behind the bird. Meanwhile, Lloyd spoke in Ggoming’s ears.

“Ggoming,” Lloyd said, “Listen carefully.”

“Ggoming?”

“You have an important role right now. You will fly to Javier while I stay here.”

“Ggomiming? Ggoming?”

“No, not the other way around. No. I’m staying here. Here.”

“Ggoming?”

“It’s all right. No matter how hard I think, this is our only option. I’ll hold that dragon here. It looks like that dragon is obsessed with me. So, I think I can do a fine job keeping it here. While I do that, you go fly to Javier.”

“Ggomiming?”

“That’s right,” affirmed Lloyd. “To Javier. You saw where he fell, right?”

“Ggoming!” exclaimed Ggoming.

“Good job. Go look for him. He should still be alive. It’s Javier after all. The Knight of Blood and Iron. Once you find him, I want you to take him and-”

“Ggoming?”

“No, don’t bring him here. Go to the city district. Take him to a doctor,” instructed Lloyd.

“Ggomiming?”

“Yes, to a doctor. You heard me right,” stressed Lloyd once again.

“Ggoming…”

“Sure, the best thing would be treating him personally,” Lloyd began to reason in a calm voice. “I can use the Asrahan Core Technique to reverberate his circles myself. But I don’t think the situation allows me to do that. That’s why it’s better for a doctor to treat him if I can’t do it myself.“

"Ggoming…”

“That will be enough. It’s Javier. He’ll be able to get better in no time. So, give him first aid and quickly carry him out. Then wait for me near the southern gate of the capital. I’ll run away there.”

“Ggomiming? Ggoming?”

“It’s all right. I don’t have any plans to fight this monster head-on. I’ll buy some time and discretely escape. So, you have nothing to worry about.”

“Ggoming…”

“I’m telling you, it’s fine!” Lloyd assured once again.

Ggoming had become sad without even realizing his feelings. Lloyd put on a smile for him. Actually, he was nervous because he wasn’t sure he could do it. But there was no other option at the moment.

This is the only way to save both Javier and me.

He hated running away alone and wanted to save Javier, his friend. The knight had never left Lloyd’s side after he arrived in this world. So, Lloyd didn’t want to lose him here or give up. That was why.

“Ggoming, I trust you!” yelled Lloyd.

“G-ggoming?!”

Lloyd let go of the saddle at the end of the soar, and his body left Ggoming’s back. Lloyd’s stomach churned with that nausea as he floated in the air just like at the peak of a Viking roller coaster. Then he began to descend.

“G-ggoming!”

The sight of Ggoming shouting in surprise grew distant, and when Lloyd looked down, his vision was filled with the nearing giant of the Bone Dragon. That was only when Lloyd regretted it.

Argh!

As the Bone Dragon filled his sight, Lloyd wondered if this was what ants felt like when they were marching toward a mountain. He had chills coming down his spine. He was scared and questioned if he had made the wrong decision.

Ugh. Are friends really this important?

He suddenly wished he just abandoned Javier and ran away. However, it was too late for regrets. Lloyd focused his mind. He opened up his arms and legs like a leaping frog, landing on one side of the Bone Dragon’s horn on its forehead and clasped his arms around the base of it.

Whack!

“Eek…!”

He almost threw up from the impact. But he succeeded in safely sticking himself onto the Bone Dragon’s horn.

Like Ggoming’s reaction, the Bone Dragon wouldn’t have seen this coming.

He had let go of his grip on the saddle without any warning. That was unexpected for Ggoming, so it must be the same for the Bone Dragon that was chasing after them. What was more, the distance between Ggoming and the Bone Dragon was extremely small at that moment.

Otherwise, the dragon would have happily eaten us in one bite.

Truth be told, he had taken a gamble. Regardless, it worked, and that was all that he cared about.

Arggh!

Lloyd embraced the horn with all of his might and tightly held on. The Bone Dragon was roughly swaying its head to shake Lloyd off after belatedly realizing what had happened. Lloyd felt like a fly stuck to a giraffe that was violently headbanging to a 16-beat rhythm.

I’ll go down for now!

He would be doomed if this continued. He would fall to the ground or be scratched to death by the dragon’s foreleg. Sensing the danger, Lloyd activated an optional skill right away.

Energizer!

Ding dong.

[The skill option of the Asrahan Core Technique, Energizer, has been activated.]

[The triple circle’s efficiency has been maximized. You can move for 20 minutes without experiencing fatigue.]

Ziiing! The circles around his heart reacted right away, the three strands rotated with greater vigor. Similar to the endless circulation of mana that was unique to sword masters, the mana amplified in the circle rotated on and on. From the first, second, third, and back to the first one again. The mana circulated as though it was being recycled, energizing his whole body with everlasting vigor. Lloyd used his strength to bear the centrifugal force the Bone Dragon created by swaying its head. Then he climbed down like a clingy sloth.

Okay!

He continued crawling down, avoiding the occasional foreleg scratches with cockroach-like instincts. He then sent a gaze to Ggoming.

Go! Hurry!

Ggoming was flying circles around him in bewilderment. Lloyd gestured at his bird friend to do what he was told. Ggoming nodded and flew away toward the point Javier fell.

Then it’s my turn now.

Lloyd decided to buy time, just enough for Ggoming to find Javier and bring him to safety.

Like this!

Lloyd doggedly crawled down, passed the dragon’s crown, and got down with its skull, where there was the socket of its eye.

Excuse me, please!

Without any second of hesitation, Lloyd entered the eye socket, crawling through the passage where the dragon’s optic nerves would have sat when it was alive. At last, he arrived at the wide cerebral ventricle. Aggressively grabbing the shovel, he swung down.

Clang! Lloyd struck the inner wall of the ventricle with his shovel, but obviously, he couldn’t leave any damage, not even a scratch. But it was more than enough to achieve his goal.

…!

Targa, the warlock in the dragon’s body, flinched. A metallic noise clanged in his head. He nearly jumped in surprise from how piercing it was.

Y-You!

Targa was flustered and enraged. He desperately wanted to rip Lloyd to pieces. That archenemy of his not only dangled on his horn but had also entered his skull and started noisily drumming the inside of it!

Get out of there now!

Targa yelled and all his bones trembled. But his shout was only said by his soul, so Lloyd didn’t hear it. Thus, it was obviously not enough to stop Lloyd.

“Ha, I like your reaction.”

One clanging strike. That was enough to make the Bone Dragon twist its whole body. Its reaction was beyond his expectation. An evil smile appeared on Lloyd’s mouth. His shovel started to dance in a more energetic fashion.

“Whew! Ha!”

Clang! Clash! Clang! Clang! Clang Clung! Clash!

S-stop…!

As the shovel became more unreserved in its movement, the Bone Dragon started to writhe and twist with more vigor. At last, it stopped fluttering its wings and landed on the ground. It wrestled to remove Lloyd by shaking and scratching its head. But Lloyd’s optional skill was activated, so he was indefatigable. Instead of getting more tired, his shoveling grew more unforgiving.

“Maybe you like the sound of nails on a chalkboard?”

…!

Screeeech! Screeeech! This time, Lloyd roughly scratched the inside of the skull with his shovel, and the Bone Dragon started to move more frantically. Targa couldn’t be any more annoyed by what was happening. But Lloyd, even though he was harassing the dragon, knew that he had to do more.

More. This isn’t enough. More.

The dragon was only momentarily flustered. It would soon get used to the scratching and beating inside its skull.

I’m not doing any real damage to it.

Lloyd was only acting as a mild annoyance to the dragon. And soon, his fears actualized when the Bone Dragon wielded its foreleg.

Boooom! Unable to bear the metallic noise any longer, the Bone Dragon smacked its head with its foreleg, the force containing thousands of tons of weight. Although the impact wasn’t enough to break its head, it was enough to give a painful blow to Lloyd.

“…!”

The thing was, even if you were riding a solid truck or a hard tank, the person inside could feel the impact of a traffic accident. The harder the object the vehicle hit, the greater the blow would be. Lloyd was experiencing just that.

“Argh!!”

The massive impact shook the whole place inside. And Lloyd’s whole body was thrown to the side, like a person in a car accident without a seat belt, and was rammed into the wall on the other end. Then he collapsed to the floor.

“…!”

He struggled to breathe. He couldn’t scream. Instead, drops of blood spluttered out of his mouth.

“Gargh..! Ha…”

Lloyd wondered if his ribs were broken. No, it felt like all the bones inside of his body were broken. He then attempted to get back to his feet, struggling as he crawled and grabbed the shovel that fell out of his hand from the blow. Just then…

Bam! He heard another blaring noise that seemed to break the sky. This time it came from the other end.

“…!”

Lloyd was hurled away once again. This time hitting the other side of the dragon’s skull. The impact knocked him unconscious, and he woke up only a few seconds later.

C-crap.

Lloyd’s whole body shivered as he grabbed the shovel. No, it wasn’t a shiver. It was a spasm. He knew he had to buy some more time, but he was out of strength. He was struggling to stay awake, let alone stand.

This is how you want to play, huh?

It never occurred to Lloyd that the Bone Dragon would smack its own head. Every body part ached as though his bones were getting crushed. He wanted to run away if he could, but it was too late. There was water under the bridge. A decision was made, and he took action. So, he had to see through the end of it. A bitter smile appeared on Lloyd’s mouth.

This is why I hated fighting.

But now that it began, he wanted to win. He wanted more than to achieve his initial goal. Now, he wanted to throw a big punch at this dragon.

Like this!

Ding dong.

The moment Lloyd hardened his resolve as he grabbed his shovel, a message that alerted the activation of an optional skill appeared in front of his eyes.

[The Asrahan Core Technique Skill Option 2: Latent Power Surge has been activated.]

[Your circle amplification rate has increased fivefold for 30 seconds.]

Ziiing! From then on, his three-stranded circles moved, wriggled, and made an uproar. They rose, roared, and went berserk. That explosive power enabled Lloyd to get to his feet. He didn’t give a hang whether he was in a critical condition or not.

I have 24 seconds.

Lloyd had enough time. Today, at this moment, he resolved to attempt everything he could within his power. Then he activated the fourth optional skill of the Asrahan Core Technique.

[The Asrahan Core Technique Skill Option 4: Circle Shift has been activated.]

Lloyd controlled the number of rotations of the frenzied three-stranded circles and adjusted them to an optimal collision ratio before reverberating them.

Ziiing! Is this what it felt like to put a leash on three roaring beasts? Driven by the Circle Shift, the three sections of the circle fought, entangled, collided, and exploded with each other. At that moment, Lloyd aimed the shovel outside of the dragon’s eye socket.

Kaboom! The output was five times more amplified than usual, so that was how much greater the collision was. The triple mana blast’s explosive power was strengthened by fivefold. Lloyd carefully shot the blast to make sure he wouldn’t be swept away by the force released in the small area. The triple mana blast went out, scratching and widening the dragon’s optic nerve passage and leaving its eye socket.

More!

One more time.

Kaboom! The triple mana blast that was amplified by fivefold lightly chiseled the bone around the dragon’s eye socket. Though the damage was small, it was enough. After all, Lloyd didn’t expect to hurt the dragon with the triple mana blast. It was nothing but preparation for the real deal, his true all-out blow. Just then…

Ding dong.

[The Asrahan Core Technique Skill Option 2: Latent Power Surge has ended.]

[As the penalty of the optional skill, you are now exhausted.]

A red message filled his vision. Strength left his body like an ebb. The frenzied circle grew suddenly quiet. But Lloyd wasn’t flustered. His eyes instead held more malice.

I told you, didn’t I? I told you to move. I’m going to try everything I can today.

So, he did everything he could. And at last, he activated it-the final move that he had planned to use from the start.

[The Asrahan Core Technique Skill Option 5: Supercharge has been activated.]

[With this option, you can indiscriminately absorb a large amount of mana from your surroundings.]

From then on, Lloyd fell into a trance-like state. His mana circle opened its eyes again, and they were the eyes of a predator. And everything within his range became prey for him to greedily absorb. And, of course, the Bone Dragon’s skull wasn’t an exemption.
