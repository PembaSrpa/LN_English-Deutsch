# How to Freeze Time (1)

---

Whoosh… The Heart of Winter had been created by Ice Dragon Tyrannus sometime long ago and presented to the House of Magentano as a gift. But it always considered itself unfortunate. Possessing overwhelming power and capabilities, it was soon locked inside a corner of the palace’s repository, away from the sun, never allowed to enjoy the outside world. As such, it was unhappy. It wanted to be treated like any other ordinary piece of treasure. If that had been the case, it wouldn’t have been tortured and imprisoned for tens of thousands of years. But it was in this predicament due to its faint consciousness and self-awareness. It felt stifled, yearned for freedom, and sought its chance. And finally, today, it found one.

Whoosh… Bathump! Bathump! The one-inch marble vibrated at a regular beat in excitement after having found the being that would unlock its shackles and free it to the outside world. This human had been drawing its interest for the past few days as the man held a heart that resembled that of a dragon’s. As such, the Heart of Winter was curious and interested in him. And today, its emotions developed into adoration. The man had been enduring the cold gusts of air blowing at him all day, and the sight propelled a greedy thought in the Heart of Winter - Why not become one with that human? As soon as that thought crossed its mind…

Whoosh… It began to emit frigid air with even more vigor than before, sending every bit of it on Lloyd. It meant to subdue Lloyd, dominate his heart, and steal his body. It would borrow his body and attain freedom. The icy storm, so indescribable and cruel, charged toward the man. Thanks to that, Lloyd felt like he was at death’s doorstep.

Ackk!!!!

Lloyd panicked. He was exhausted after having moved one magician, six knights, and nineteen burly men out of the cave. The trip out even involved climbing up a 147-foot ladder while carrying each of them on his back.

It was no wonder he was exhausted. He gave almost all his mana to the people he carried on his back through the Asrahan Core Technique, leaving barely enough for him to use. His brute muscle strength and endurance were the only things he could rely on to conduct the rescue, so his wobbly legs were about to give way anytime soon. That was when the situation went south as he was met with a storm of ice.

Dammit! Why is it getting colder?!

A pang of alarm rushed through his heart. His stomach dropped like a heavy pack of ice on the floor. Just then, a thought flashed across his mind. He was going to die if he didn’t do anything now.

I can’t faint here.

Lloyd bit his lips. Of all the places, he had to be right in front of the refrigeration room. What bad luck. It was the closest place to the rampageous Heart of Winter and the coldest area in the entire whole cave. What if he lost strength, fell to the ground, and passed out here? What would happen?

I’ll be done for.

The worst-case scenario flashed through his exhausted mind.

I’ll be exposed to the freezing cold while I’m knocked out. I won’t die, still. I won’t get hypothermia or freeze to death. I have the effect with me. But will I be okay? No. Of course I won’t.

His whole body would be frostbitten from the hours of exposure to the icy air. In particular, his face and hands, which were not protected by clothes, would take the biggest hit.

My skin will freeze and be ruined. The same goes for the muscles underneath. And all that tissue will necrotize.

In no exaggeration, his skin would rot. He would be left with comprehensive and serious skin damage. As a result, it would give him permanent, untreatable side effects.

I don’t want that.

Lloyd shuddered. He could end up needing his hands chopped off. His face would be completely ruined. In all seriousness, he could really be left to live in hellish pain as he suffered from various side effects from his skin injury if things turned for the worse.

That can’t happen.

Lloyd shook his head, striving to pull himself together. But it turned out to be harder than he wanted it to be. His depleted stamina and mana. The Asrahan Core Technique that was in energy conservation mode. None of it could help him. And his consciousness continued to fade until his knees eventually crumpled. He reached out his hands, fighting for his life. He touched the ground. At that moment…

“Ouch…!”

His right palm ached, and when he reflexively flinched, he noticed his palm was red with blood.

“…”

Could it be that he was pierced by an ice shard? However, the pain cleared his mind for a second, and he realized something.

This could be my last chance.

Soon, he was certainly going to lose consciousness again amidst the raging ice storm. He’d fade away until he completely passed out. That wasn’t something he could allow to happen.

I need to do something. Anything.

Lloyd made a judgment. He then calculated, predicted, decided, and put it all into action.

Ding dong.

[The Asrahan Core Technique Skill Option ⑤: Supercharge has been activated.]

[You will randomly absorb a large amount of mana from your surroundings.]

Lloyd activated his Supercharge. He clenched his teeth.

This is the best I can do right now.

Lloyd was convinced that the Supercharge skill, which let him absorb mana from his surroundings like a blackhole, would suck in large amounts of the frigid air being emitted by the Heart of Winter.

Then, the cold air that’s absorbed will have an effect on my manaheart or my circle. Just as it has until now. Similar to when it stole mana from the queen, undead Mastodons, Hell’s Knight, and the Bone Dragon. A new skill option will appear, and it’ll give me more parameters to work with.

His new parameters would be helpful for him. No, even if it didn’t, he didn’t care.

At least, my empty manaheart will be full.

Supercharge. This would get his empty manaheart to restore itself again. And he’d be able to recharge himself and revitalize his worn-out muscles. It would allow him to squeeze out enough strength to save Ppodong, Bangul, and the group leader.

So… Please, work!

Lloyd wished for it with all of his heart. With that wish, his consciousness fell into a trance-like state and faded away as his three-strand mana circle began to roar away. Ziiing!

A swirling storm of mana formed around him, and it soon started to devour all the mana nearby. It absorbed everything it could. The concrete floor, the lock bolts, and the rocks surrounding the area. The ice storm was no exception.

Whoosh… At the sudden change of events, the Heart of Winter wondered in shock if the man had finally lost it. It had sent the ice storm to the man to steal his heart, only to have him actively absorb the storm. The Heart of Winter wondered if death was indeed what he wanted. But soon, it learned that wasn’t the case.

Bzzt…

It turned out that the human was absorbing and processing the cold air for his own use, digesting the mana loaded in the air and recharging his manaheart.

Bzzt!

The Heart of Winter broke into a fit of laughter. This human was getting more interesting the more it learned about him. At the same time, it pitied him. It was like a giant in the presence of a child trying to defend himself with a toothpick. Even though Lloyd was desperately absorbing the cold air, the amount he was taking in was quite insignificant. It was like trying to scoop up a giant iceberg with a single spoon.

Amused, the Heart of Winter decided to dance to his tune. Let him try as much as he wanted. Let him absorb as much air as his heart desired. It proceeded to produce a much more powerful ice storm. The volume of it easily surpassed what Lloyd could take in. The result was destructive.

Bzzt!! Whoosh… Whoosh! Lloyd’s body was soon covered with frost. His skin started to freeze, and his exposed face and hands started to develop frostbite. The cold air that his Supercharge failed to absorb began to damage his skin. It was a devastatingly pitiful fight. No, it wasn’t even a fight. It was a futile effort of resistance. A hopeless struggle, a protest that was bound to fail. And just like that, Lloyd’s whole body was wrapped together with frost and ice. It was around the time when his lower torso was confined by a pillar of ice that Javier arrived.

“Master Lloyd…?”

Javier, who managed to break through the cold and get to the bottom of the cave, was flabbergasted. He was in the middle of training with the queen when he left her to arrive here. A sense of dread had gripped him the entire time he made his journey. He immediately rushed to the cave after hearing a brief explanation of the situation. He wished and prayed for the fake Lloyd’s safety.

“…”

But when he arrived, he found Lloyd half stuck in the ice pillar. He immediately learned what was going on.

It’ll be hopeless if this continues.

The Asrahan Core Technique allowed him to see the flow of events that occurred and enabled him to feel the mana absorption in Lloyd. Javier learned that Lloyd was aggressively absorbing mana around him. But there was too much mana for Lloyd to suck in. That was why he was losing and being trapped by the cold air he couldn’t absorb.

When Javier realized that, he walked forward without delay into the Supercharge range. And he made himself the target.

Boom! A large volume of mana started to leak from Javier’s manaheart from all of his cells. And yet, he didn’t mind. He simply responded with his Asrahan Core Technique, reversing the rotation of his three-strand circle and altering the flow of Lloyd’s Supercharge. This allowed him to be unaffected by the mana absorption. But Javier didn’t stop there.

He took one more step toward Lloyd and reached out and touched Lloyd’s shoulder. Closing his eyes, he focused on his manaheart, waking his three-strand mana circle and jumping onto Lloyd’s mana circle flow. And just like that, Javier and Lloyd’s mana circles were connected, and they started to go wild. Javier’s three strands. Lloyd’s three strands. Now, there was one six-strand mana circle. It began to rotate, resonate, and amplify. The effect was three times three. In total, the amplification of nine strands. The explosive vitality and overwhelming absorption began to shred, tear, and devour everything.

Ziiing! Now, the absorption had reached a new height of strength. It was the Supercharge of the nine-strand mana circle. Adding to that, Javier took one more step and touched Lloyd’s back, this time connecting his manaheart to Lloyd’s. Lloyd’s intermediate-level manaheart in his chest, and Javier’s grandmaster-level manaheart made of 60 trillion cells. One big lump. Separated into 60 trillion pieces. The two contrasting manahearts synced, linked, and responded until they eventually formed a multi-manaheart system. The new system assisted the nine-strand mana circle. It digested and purified the mana from the Supercharge and made it into his own. One by one. Like filling up an empty barn. Without a single drop of mana missed. Faithfully yet violently. Ferociously but meticulously. It dominated, conquered, and reigned. Meanwhile, Javier thought to himself…

I do not understand you.

His gaze traveled to Lloyd’s back. Lloyd didn’t respond to Javier’s touch and help as he was in a trance. Lloyd. The eldest son of the Frontera family. However, he was a fraud. An intruder from another world. Despite that, he was the sole pillar holding up the family. The only source of hope that could save the count and countess from the impending disaster. It was the reason why Javier was willingly helping him at the moment.

What you think about the family, the lord, and about me. I cannot tell if you are using us. Whether that is why you have been hiding your identity this entire time. I do not know the answer to anything. However…

However… Javier had only one wish.

I hope I won’t come to regret the countless number of times I made the decision to help you in the past and in the present.

Javier focused his mind more. He concentrated every bit of his strength on the mana circle and manaheart now linked with Lloyd. Lloyd, too, instinctively responded to Javier by absorbing and devouring everything. He didn’t care if the Heart of Winter was a divine item or an artifact created by a dragon. He simply absorbed, devoured, possessed, and conquered. And eventually, the Heart of Winter began to lose out to the tyrannous Supercharge.

Whoosh…

The Heart of Winter vibrated with more consistency, intrigued to meet the human capable of suppressing it. This human had been piquing its interest for days now because he carried a heart akin to a dragon’s heart. But now, its interest developed into feelings of admiration.

Even though he was receiving help from another person, it was he who was devouring its ice storm. And a new impulse came over it: What about becoming a part of that human? What if it intentionally became a part of him and watched over the world? That sounded feasible. And the man seemed pretty qualified to be its owner. If that was the case, there wasn’t any need to be bothered to dominate and pilot the human.

Whoosh… It started to bring out every bit of its essence and push it all toward Lloyd when his absorption was at its peak. It did so to assimilate into his heart, become a part of him, and hopefully experience freedom in the future. It did so as if it was offering a handshake. In an intentional, friendly, polite, and careful manner. Lloyd’s manaheart happily took the handshake.

Whoosh… The Heart of Winter. The divine item that held the strength of one season. Lloyd’s manaheart, which was marvelously absorbing everything with the grandmaster’s help. At that moment, two strange existences turned into one.

Ding dong.

[The Asrahan Core Technique Skill Option ⑤: Your Supercharge time is up.]

Ziiing… Lloyd’s violent mana circle calmed down. The ferocious rotation quietened, and the cold air swirling around him was now gone. Every bit of it. And so was the Heart of Winter, the divine item belonging to the royal house. In addition, a new message appeared in front of Lloyd’s eyes.

[You have absorbed the Heart of Winter, a divine item that possesses a consciousness.]

[The strength of the winter season inside the item seeped into your manaheart. This experience is having a strong effect on your manaheart skill.]

[A new skill option has been opened.]

[Manaheart Skill Option ⑤: Time Freeze]
