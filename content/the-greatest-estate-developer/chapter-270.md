# How to Treat an Upset Stomach (2)

---

“The best thing to do when you have an upset stomach is to mercilessly prick your fingertip to let the blood out!”

“…”

Bubble… A current of cold water swooshed past Lloyd and Queen Kiakora, its bubbles awkwardly drifting upward. The queen burst out laughing.

“Ha!” Dismay came over her. What did she just hear? But her ears were fine. What she just heard was utterly unbelievable. “Prick… a finger?” stammered the queen.

“Yes.” Lloyd confidently nodded. “The thing is, when you’re having an upset stomach, you’ve got to tie your finger and prick it with a needle!”

“And with a needle?!” The queen looked at her finger in greater confusion. “Fingers? I should prick them?”

“Yes,” answered Lloyd casually.

“Will any finger do?”

“No.” Lloyd shook his head. Pointing to the tip of his finger, he gravely said, “Do you see here? This is where the root is. Stab it! Give a prick! Then…”

“Then what?”

“You will see blood. Dark blood.”

“And after that?” Her voice was still filled with doubt.

“Remove the thread from your fingers and rub the back. That will cause it to burp.”

“But the Kraken already burped earlier-”

“It’s different,” Lloyd politely explained. “How do I say it? It’s this deep burp that comes from within.”

Gaping at Lloyd, Queen Kiakora seriously deliberated whether this human being was a quack from the land or a malicious imposter. But she soon shook her head, pushing her bad thoughts away.

No. He was the only one who knew that indigestion was the reason behind this mess.

On top of that, this was coming from a guy who had experience with this matter. The queen decided to believe in him a little more. She would remain skeptical but hear him out. Having decided, she asked, “Okay, let us say that the upset stomach will be gone by… pricking one of its fingers with a needle. But we are talking about the Kraken here. It does not have any hands. Do you plan to prick the ends of its legs?”

“Yes.” Lloyd grinned and continued to speak. “In all honesty, needling your hand in the case of an upset stomach isn’t a medically proven practice. It is just a home remedy, or perhaps, a treatment that triggers the placebo effect.”

“What is the placebo effect?”

“It’s when a treatment doesn’t have any medical effectiveness but rather affects the mind. You psychologically believe something works, and your condition gets healed because of that positive mindset,” explained Lloyd.

“Hold on. Then…”

“That’s right,” Lloyd finished her sentence. “Your Majesty should inform the Kraken beforehand about the effects of needling its legs in the most positive and confident manner possible.”

“So,” summarized the queen. “We need to prick the tips of its legs and hope the placebo effect will help.”

“That is correct.” Lloyd was relieved. The queen seemed to follow exactly what he instructed.

She is out of options, probably.

Since the merpeople were said to never get an upset stomach, Lloyd assumed that they wouldn’t know how painful it was to have an upset stomach.

And they won’t know that refreshing feeling you get in your stomach as it settles when you prick your finger.

Countless people dismissed this fingertip needling as a placebo effect. A host of media outlets and doctors on TV reiterated that it has no actual benefits. But there were quite a number of people in South Korea who experienced its effect, and Lloyd was one of them.

It works for sure. I experienced it myself.

On the first night of his high school trip, the night he accidentally took a swig of soju to relieve his stomach, Lloyd spent the whole night rushing to the bathroom and retching. Unable to ignore him, his friends reported him to their teacher, and even when she recognized the alcoholic smell, she showed a surprising reaction. Instead of lashing out at his friends, she went down to the lobby and brought a thread and needle to prick Lloyd’s finger.

Actually, I had no idea what she was going to do even as she tied up my finger. I thought it was a useless treatment. But it turned out to be effective.

Dark blood streamed out from his finger, and as she loosened the thread, she stroked his back. Then came the deepest and most refreshing burp he ever experienced. At the same time, the big lump in his stomach dissipated. It was a feeling that Lloyd could not forget, even to this day.

So, it might work this time too. No, it has to work.

What would happen if the Kraken commotion continued until it ended in an armed battle with the Mermaid Corps? There was a high chance the jewel lot would be destroyed. Lloyd wished to avoid such mishaps from happening at all costs. Reminding himself of this, he suggested, “To be honest, I am unsure as to which leg we must prick. So, I say we prick them all.”

“All? All eight of them?”

“Yes,” stressed Lloyd, “You will have to tie up all of its eight legs and prick them at the same time when you see my signal.”

“Um…” Queen Kiakora faltered.

“It won’t die. And it’s not like something terrible will happen. Its upset stomach will be much more agonizing and painful than a couple of pricks.”

“Tsk. Fine. Let us give it a try.” Eventually, she conceded to his plan.

Still, his method will be better than suppressing the Kraken.

It’d be the case both for the Kraken and the merpeople. The queen summoned the generals under her command and quickly relayed the operation plan.

“That is all. Like I said, divide yourselves into seventeen units when you start to move. Am I clear?”

“Yes, my queen.”

“However, never put yourselves at risk or push yourselves too hard. When you sense danger, you must command your merpeople to retreat. Got it?”

“Your wish is my command.”

The generals nodded before returning to their respective units. From that moment, the merpeople busily kickstarted their operation. The first one to swim was the queen, who hurtled toward the Kraken as she yelled that help was on the way and endured the treatment even if it was painful. That it would prick for a bit, but it’s all for its sake. She drew the octopus’s attention as she yelled aloud. Meanwhile, the Mermaid Corps jumped into action.

Веер! Веер! A high-frequency sound reminiscent of a whistle blow rang aloud. It was a signal used among the units to communicate with one another. The Corps was subdivided into seventeen units. Up, down, left, and right. East, west, south, and north. The operation took place as each unit moved in several different directions.

“Sisters! Aim for the ends of its legs!”

“Remember! Tie up the tip!”

“Use your hair!”

“Tighten the knot!”

The merpeople plucked a handful of hair before tying and connecting to form eight hair ropes that stretched hundreds of feet. The eight units in charge of the rope swam closer toward the legs of the Kraken.

“Grab tight!”

“Hold it!”

Hundreds of merpeople clutched the tip of the Kraken’s legs, each of them exerting a massive amount of strength to slow its movements. While this happened, the hair ropes were wrapped around the ends of its legs.

“Tie it!”

“Pull!” It formed a knot.

“Kru-rururung!” The Kraken writhed once more at the restrictive feeling. “Krung!”

Like a child struggling to not get his finger pricked, the Kraken roared ferociously and wielded all of its legs. But the merpeople didn’t just stay still and do nothing.

“Needle!” The queen’s command prompted the eight units in charge of the needle to advance. Their needle was the horn of the Gigatitan stored in the weaponry egg.

“Charge!”

Ten merpeople were assigned to each horn, and they swam in full force at a daunting speed. The horn of the Gigatitan glinted sharply and pricked eight legs of the sea monster.

Just then, the queen shouted, “Twist and pull out!”

They needed to give a clean prick but slightly twist the needle as they pulled out so more blood would come out.

Thwack! The giant needle that pierced 6.5 feet deep was pulled out after a twist, incising a slab of the Kraken’s skin. Given its humongous size, however, it wasn’t a serious cut. It was just a little sting.

“Krung!”

The Kraken pulled its legs close to its body, taken aback by the simultaneous stinging pain. The merpeople loosened the rope, and it started to bleed

Splash! Supposedly, the Kraken’s blood was blue. But now, its color was…

“It’s murky and dark!” One of the merpeople shouted. Every merperson quickly drew back as they observed the Kraken, which was violently wielding its legs. The color of the blood spilling out of them was darker than usual. The sight brought an eruption of cheer from the merpeople.

“We succeeded!”

The operation worked. It was just as the land human said, dark blood would come out of the Kraken after nicking the tips of its legs.

I never thought such an outrageous solution would work.

But the operation was yet to be finished. The Kraken still needed to burp.

Knowing this well, the queen adamantly yelled, “Unit seventeen!”

At her call, unit seventeen instantly took action. They brought over 30 emperor whales that they had hurriedly gathered from a nearby sea.

“Approach!”

The whales swam at the guidance of the merpeople, and though they were a little scared, they approached the Kraken from behind since the request was coming from the merpeople. The whales then clung to the Kraken’s skin. Their bodies, which were often mistaken by humans as the head of an octopus, drew closer to the octopus.

“Rub!”

They diligently rubbed the Kraken with their entire body, caressing its belly and back. The octopus’s face relaxed a little.

“K-Krurung…”

Its voice softened, and its eyes relaxed when…

Buuuuurp! The Kraken let out a deep and intense burp that echoed through the area. The clear, ringing sound coming from the orchestra that was its intestinal villi seemed to spread across the entire world.

“It’s over…”

The queen clenched her fists. The plan actually worked. The echoing burp wouldn’t have come out otherwise. The Kraken wouldn’t look as relaxed otherwise.

We did it.

The Kraken should feel more comfortable now. So, the only thing left for them was to comfort their friend and gather up the scattered eggs to rebuild the city. The bottled-up tension finally vanished in the queen’s heart. And beaming brightly, she went back to Lloyd to compliment his great contribution. But she found him still grimacing.

This isn’t over yet. It didn’t burp enough.

In contrast to the merpeople who were cheering and celebrating their success, Lloyd was frowning. There were deep wrinkles forming between his brows. His gaze on the Kraken was grave. The reason was simple.

Its stomach is still upset.

The merpeople wouldn’t know, but he knew because he had used his surveying skill on the monster. He was able to take a look sixteen feet deep into its body, although the Kraken was so large that he still couldn’t get past its thick, leathery skin. Nonetheless, Lloyd still observed the octopus’s skin movement and blood flow. That was how he knew.

Ever since it’s been suffering from an upset stomach, I noticed a mild, irregular convulsion in its skin. And it’s still happening right now. The burping stopped the convulsions for a bit, but it’s starting again.

Lloyd instantly knew. The operation was yet to be finished.

It worked, sure. But the plan was only about 98% successful.

Lloyd estimated that the leg needling and burping weren’t enough to relieve the Kraken’s pain due to how severe its indigestion was. Lloyd, thus, decided to take a drastic measure.

Since the pricking and the burping didn’t work, I’m left with no other choice. I need to make it vomit.

Time was running out, it seemed. The Kraken was close to being rampant once again. It would be too late by then. It was now or never. There was no time to inform or explain anything to the others. Sensing that, Lloyd immediately threw his hand into his pocket and brought Bangul out.

“Hey, Bangul,” said Lloyd to Bangul, who was perched on his palm.

“Bangul?”

“I’m sorry, but I think you should use your volcanic explosion for us.”

“Bba-bangul?”

“Over there. With me on your back. Do you think you can do it?”

“Bangul!” Bangul earnestly nodded, so Lloyd fed her red sunflower seeds and hopped on her back once she grew larger. Bangul began gulping the water and wagged her tail.

“Bangul! Bba-bangul!”

Jingle! Jingle! Her warning rattle rang aloud in the water, and right after that, she raised her tail, discharging a massive explosion. “Bangul!”

Boooom! Volcanic explosion with massive strength. The force sent Bangul away like a rocket, and Lloyd, too, experienced the same thing as he was on her back.

“Argh…!

A terrible current of water hit his face and body. He ducked down as low as he could see and checked his target. His gaze focused on the Kraken, and he zoomed in on the middle of its forehead, which he learned from the surveying skill was the connecting point between its head and stomach. Lloyd pointed toward its direction.

"Hey, Bangul!”

“Bangul!”

“Do you see where I’m pointing at?! Toward the middle part of its forehead! One more time!” shrieked Lloyd as the adrenaline started to pump in him.

“Bba-bangul!”

Boooom! Two volcanic explosions consecutively erupted in the water!

“…!”

Lloyd was thrown at a greater speed, and the water current hit him much harsher. But Lloyd didn’t lose his focus. At the final moment, he reversed his shovel, leaped from Bangul’s back, and fired the triple manablast.

Kaboom! The third explosion propelled him farther, and he was now a torpedo flying toward the forehead of the Kraken. With that immense speed, he rammed right into its forehead.

Thwack! Lloyd managed to lay an attack right at his desired target, the Kraken’s soft and thick forehead. Anatomically, it was the point that adjoins its head and stomach. The human equivalent of it would be the pit of one’s stomach. There. That was where the third rocket propulsion landed. The effect of it was impressive.

Blergh! The Kraken convulsed and vomited something in a flash.

Retch! A big lump of emerald sculpture, which resonated at the same frequency as the Jewel of Truth, emerged.
