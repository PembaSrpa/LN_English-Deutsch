# What Goes On Inside the Dragon (1)

---

Dang. I hit the jackpot.

Lloyd’s eyes widened. He carefully read the messages that appeared in front of him with an incredulous heart.

The Bone Dragon is dying? It’s almost out of HP and suffering from terrible exhaustion as it’s depleted of mana. And so, this is saying the condition for my Zombie Domination is satisfied for now.

Lloyd decided to take his chance, so he quickly opened the skill window and floated the details of the Zombie Domination skill in front of his eyes.

Ding dong.

[Skill Name: Zombie Domination]

[Stage: Basic Lv.1]

[You can dominate a set scale of undead soldiers. The undead soldiers under your domination will provide unconditional allegiance and faithfulness to you.]

[Types of undead soldiers you can dominate: zombies and skeletons.]

[The number of undead soldiers you can dominate: 200]

[The current number of undead soldiers under your domination: 200]

[Required RP to level up: 50]

[Current RP: 2,632]

Hmm, the skill details that only the zombies and skeletons can be dominated. But how is it that I can dominate the Bone Dragon now? Wait, does that mean…

Lloyd postulated that it could be because the Bone Dragon was in a critical state, quite literally with its low HP. So, it was close to something like a zombie or skeleton for the time being. That was how he satisfied the Zombie Domination condition. If that was the case, there was no time to lose.

Whatever the reason is, it means that my skill will work. I’d be a fool to miss this chance.

Money on the floor was meant to be taken. A free-tasting sample was to be tasted. Coupons were to be collected. Perhaps, it was a little cheap and pathetic. But that was how Suho Kim survived in South Korea. Lloyd moved right away and activated the Zombie Domination skill, throwing his gaze at the remnants of the Bone Dragon cut up into thousands of pieces. But…

Ding dong.

[Zombie Domination has failed.]

[Error Code: 00A1, Capacity Limit]

[There is no space to accept another undead entity. Either fire an entity you are currently dominating or expand your span of domination.]

Dang. What a novice mistake.

Lloyd scolded himself for being too impatient. Hurriedly, Lloyd opened the skill window.

The sweet reward in front of me was so great, I lost my cool for a second. I can’t act like this. Pull yourself together, Lloyd.

He invested his RP, and the result came out right away.

[Skill Level Up!]

[Skill Name: Zombie Domination]

[Stage: Basic Lv. 2]

[Types of undead soldiers you can dominate: zombies and skeletons.]

[The number of undead soldiers you can dominate: 210]

[The current number of undead soldiers under your domination: 200]

[Required RP to level up: 70]

[Current RP: 2,582]

Perfect.

Now, he had enough capacity. Lloyd’s gaze moved restlessly toward the Bone Dragon, his eyes containing conviction and greed. He activated the Zombie Domination skill right away, and the reaction came immediately.

Ding dong.

[Zombie Domination skill has been activated.]

[The designated entity, the remains of the Bone Dragon, has started to react to the skill.]

[1,963 pieces of its skeleton have been recruited minus one.]

[Among the 1,963 pieces, one piece is resisting the skill.]

Hmm?

Lloyd lifted his brow when he learned that over 1,900 pieces of the Dragon Bone were recruited except for one, and it was under protest.

What is this now?

Lloyd was afraid that his skill would be disabled while he wasted time like this. The mana of the Bone Dragon could recover its health level above that of zombies and skeletons.

Then I won’t meet the condition.

Lloyd’s neck broke out in a cold sweat as the chance to turn the Bone Dragon into his minion seemed to fade away. If that happened, it’d be the same as cooking a delicious pot of soup, only to spill all of it before serving by slipping.

That won’t do.

Lloyd’s eyes hurried. Just in time, a new message popped up.

[Would you like to see the details for the protesting piece?]

[YES / NO]

Of course, it’s a yes!

[Loading the details…]

Ding dong.

With a refreshing ding, a wall of information cascaded in front of him, and Lloyd quickly moved his eyes like a person going through the monthly account history of their credit card

[The piece of the bone that is currently protesting against the Zombie Domination is identified as a parietal bone.]

[As the result of the scanning, a soul leeching in the bone has been detected.]

[The leeching soul is resisting the Zombie Domination.]

[To view the information on the soul, please select “Here.”]

Lloyd selected it without hesitation. And the information on the soul that was protesting to Lloyd’s skill and leeching in the Bone Dragon’s parietal bone was revealed.

Ding dong.

[Leeching Soul]

[Name: Targa]

[Gender: Male]

[Age: 47]

[Occupation: Warlock]

[Height: 5'8]

[Weight: 134 lbs]

[Other information: Low blood pressure, neurotic indigestion, balding hair, salt tooth, sauce pourer, unmarried, forever single, ambitious, leader of the warlock organization, and killed in the hands of Queen Magentano while planning a host of treasonous schemes.]

“…”

Aha, Lloyd exclaimed inwardly. The leader of the warlocks was here. The information on the soul that floated in front of his eyes gave him an insight at last.

This case was like the Trojan Horse.

Lloyd recalled how the queen had suppressed and defeated the warlocks, who perished without even having a second to respond. The suppression was said to be much easier than everyone had expected, and it ended with the queen slicing the head of the leader. But the queen must have missed something in the process.

This warlock must have transferred his soul into the Dragon Bone right before he died.

Just like the Trojan Horse. Just like how the Achaean army hid its elite soldiers in the Trojan’s Horse for the ambush, the warlock must have hidden his own soul in the Bone Dragon.

And that was why he was obsessed with me.

Lloyd recalled how the Bone Dragon ran riot and laser-focused on him for some strange reason. He had no idea back then. But now, once the identity of the soul was enlightened, Lloyd understood.

He must’ve thought I put a brake on his plans in Namaran. Tsk. This is the problem with the villains. They blame others when the fault lies in them.

Lloyd moved his eyes, this time more relaxed. And he continued to read down the messages.

Ding dong.

[The soul living in the parietal bone has been identified.]

[You can eliminate the identified soul at your will.]

[Would you like to eliminate?]

[YES / NO]

Of course, it’s a yes.

There was no reason for hesitation. Lloyd nodded right away. Just then, he heard a faint shout coming from the parietal bone.

“H-hold on!”

Hmm?

The voice seemed to belong to a sly, insufferable old man. Lloyd figured it was Targa. His voice continued shouting in a more hurried tone.

“Hold on! Don’t do this! Listen to me!”

“Hello? What did you just say?”

“Yes! Give me a chance!”

“A chance?”

Lloyd snorted as he shrugged his shoulders. And he gave Targa, who was begging in desperation, a belittling laugh.

“Why are you asking me to give you a chance?”

“What…?”

“Ask who’ll judge you, not me.”

“H-hold on!”

Clack. Lloyd selected “YES” without any hesitation. And then came the shouting of Targa, whose scream sounded like a flushing toilet. Unsurprisingly, he was soon met with a joyful message.

[The soul of Warlock Targa living in the parietal bone of the Bone Dragon has been eliminated.]

[Now, all of the 1,963 broken bones of the Bone Dragon are reacting to your Zombie Domination.]

[The Zombie Domination skill has succeeded.]

[The Bone Dragon is now yours.]

Yes! This is what I’m talking about!

It was a success. Lloyd clenched his fists because he now held control over the Bone Dragon. But there was one more process left before actualizing it, and that was the restoration. Thankfully, Lloyd didn’t have to put a lot of effort into it.

[The Bone Dragon attempts to restore its body in accordance with its “Immortal” nature.]

[Its mana inside is gradually being restored.]

[The broken bones are piecing together.]

Crack… Crack… The shattered mass of the Bone Dragon, which was the masterful work of Javier, began to tumble on the ground. It started to absorb the surrounding mana, like the Asrahan Core Technique. And just like that, the Bone Dragon restored itself on its own, putting together all of its broken parts and returning to its original shape. Meanwhile, Lloyd struggled to stop Javier as the latter grabbed his sword again when the bones jerked.

“Wait! Stop!” yelled Lloyd in a rush. “That’s mine now.”

“That… is yours now, Master Lloyd?” asked Javier, his voice breaking in disbelief.

“Uh-huh.”

Javier looked at the dragon and back at Lloyd, his sword pulled out of the sheath just midway. There was curiosity and dismay in his gaze.

“You may have not heard him,” Lloyd hurriedly threw an explanation to convince Javier. “The Bone Dragon just spoke to my heart.”

“What did it tell you? What?”

“The baby of the Skeleton Corps. That is, it wants to enter as the newest member.”

“…”

“You beat it to a pulp,” coolly explained Lloyd, “so it surrendered.”

“Is that true?”

“Sure is. Look.”

Lloyd lifted his one hand, making a thumbs-up gesture and looking at the Bone Dragon. The Bone Dragon in the middle of the restoration reacted and made the same gesture.

“Did you see that?” said Lloyd.

“…”

“I’ll elaborate on the details later on. First, we have to restore it to one piece. Got it?”

“Yes… I see…”

Javier was at a loss for words. The Bone Dragon had been wreaking havoc on everything just a moment ago, and he had ripped the creature into shreds. So, Javier wasn’t easily persuaded when Lloyd said that he recruited the dragon and got it to enlist in the Skeleton Corps. It felt unreal even though he was looking right at it.

But it’s evidently a fact.

Javier could feel it by alternately looking at Lloyd and the Bone Dragon. In just a matter of seconds, its whole body became intact as its broken bones and dislocated joints started to come together. The Bone Dragon was now bowing obediently at Lloyd. What could be his secret? Javier mused. There was no way of knowing. Curiosity, reverence, and confusion appeared in his eyes as he looked at Lloyd. While he was thinking over these things, Lloyd was focusing on the restoration.

Okay, the restoration is going well.

Lest the assembling goes wrong with a missing bone part, Lloyd carefully observed the process as though he was choosing the best pork cut to treat himself or observing the pack of potatoes he selected after much deliberation or selecting the biggest bread in the rack. Lloyd’s eyes shone sharply.

[The Bone Dragon’s body has been restored.]

[The regenerated Bone Dragon now owns a new personality.]

[Its new personality is like a blank page.]

[From now on, the words that you tell will have a significant influence on its new personality.]

Messages cascaded in front of his eyes. Lloyd realized right away that this moment marked the final process and the most crucial moment in having the Bone Dragon under him. Catching on it, Lloyd quickly put on his thinking cap and beat his brain for the things to say. And then, he spoke.

I am Lloyd Frontera, your absolute owner and the most respectable and reliable creature in this world. Moreover, you are a proud member of the Skeleton Corps I manage. You are an excavator that says, “How deep?” when I say, “Dig.” You are a crane and so much more. A construction machine is perfect in all ways. So, you enjoy labor all the time. Chores make you happy. And safety comes first. Hurray, big hurray for Master Lloyd. If you get what I’m saying, shoot me a finger heart.

Lloyd sent those strong thoughts to the Bone Dragon as he looked at it. The Bone Dragon reacted immediately.

Rumble…! It lifted its body and spread out its wings. And then, it raised its foreleg in the most polite manner and made a finger heart. At the same time, a refreshing message appeared in front of Lloyd’s eyes.

Ding dong.

[Your Zombie Domination on the Bone Dragon succeeded.]

[From now on, the Bone Dragon will become a faithful worker and dutifully perform all of your commands.]

[The list of your Zombie Domination has been updated.]

[The number of undead soldiers you can dominate: 210]

[The current number of undead soldiers under your domination: 201 (↑1)]

Nice. Super nice!

It was done. The dragon was even officially registered. Thus, the giant Bone Dragon was his minion. After confirming the fact, an acute desire to dance swelled over him. But just then, another unexpected message appeared in front of him.

Ding dong.

[Manaheart Skill Option 4: Half-Human Half-Dragon has been activated.]

[The dragon soul nearby started to show interest in you.]

Eh?

Lloyd wondered what was happening.

A dragon soul is interested in me?

Confusion flashed across his face. The next moment, a voice of the soul recognizable only to Lloyd came from the Bone Dragon, who still was making the “I love you” with its fingers.

“I am the dragon from ancient times… I am Anticus… The original owner of this skeleton… Which is now yours… If you do me a favor… I will report to the Dragon King of your good deed… Please… Help… Me…”
