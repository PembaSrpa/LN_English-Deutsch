# A Special Host (2)

---

“I hereby stand before you to make a declaration in everyone’s presence.” Queen Magetano’s voice boomed, reaching the corners of the banquet hall. Everyone’s gaze followed where she pointed - Lloyd was standing there.

“As I revealed tonight, the Bone Dragon has become a loyal subject of the House of Magentano,” the queen went on. “However, this great achievement is not mine or that of the imperial family. I am here to reveal the praiseworthy individual whose identity has remained hidden from everyone.”

All the top foreign diplomats, nobles, and other prominent figures frowned when they heard this. Each of them started to think carefully about what that could mean. The queen continued.

“The contributors are Lloyd Frontera, whom you are looking at, and his personal guard, Knight Javier Asrahan. The day the Bone Dragon attacked us, these two men bravely took action first, drawing their swords and standing shoulder to shoulder with me on the battlefield.”

Her words meant that the two men had joined her in a fight against the Bone Dragon. Astonishment and awe filled everyone’s eyes.

“Lloyd Frontera, in particular, did not hesitate to risk his life to protect me, and he accomplished the incredible feat of suppressing the Bone Dragon. Thus, I hereby declare…”

Everyone looked from the queen to Lloyd, their ears perked up to catch what the queen had to say.

“Lloyd Frontera, the eldest son of the Frontera county, shall be a meritorious retainer of the House of Magentano. He shall be granted the privilege of legal immunity. He shall not be arrested in dishonor in any event. My judgment, rather than that of other nobles, shall be the sole determiner to decree a punishment for him.”

Everyone at the scene, the foreign diplomats and powerful nobles of the kingdom, gaped in shock as fright filled their eyes. The designation as a meritorious retainer and the right to legal immunity. Both of these were extraordinary.

Their astonishment made sense. The designation was rarely ever given. It was bestowed only to those who accomplished something equal to helping the founding of a nation, restoring power to a deteriorating throne, saving an imperial family from downfall, or preventing the kingdom from destruction. Simply put, only those who saved the kingdom could merit such an appointment.

As such, the authorities vested in those individuals were extremely powerful. The title alone awarded them the treatment equal to a duke or marquess. In addition, a host of other privileges, too many to enumerate, were granted to them. The best had to be the privilege of legal immunity, which immunized them from dishonorable arrests even for grave charges such as treason. And they also received the right to be exempted from the adjudication of nobles. As such, the monarch had to step in for the punishment of meritorious retainers.

This clearly meant one thing. Now, Lloyd was below one person but above everyone. He was the second most powerful figure in the nation apart from the monarch. No one had earned this stature for the past 130 years in the Magentano Kingdom. He was the first.

What’s happening…?

The diplomats’ heads were now in a muddle. The designation was as breaking news as the subjugation of the Bone Dragon because now, the center of power among the Magentano nobles was about to shift. Such a future seemed to loom over them as they racked their brains on how they ought to format their messages back home. But they weren’t the only ones overwhelmed by these thoughts and concerns. The nobles were also troubled.

Haha. Hahaha.

Various emotions gripped them. Lloyd Frontera. He used to be nothing but the eldest son of a noble family in a remote land of the east. A nobody these major nobles paid no attention to and never heard or took an interest in. But this had changed in the past few years.

Starting with the Llo-Javi suspension bridge, he saved the queen from being assassinated. And in the process, news broke out that his personal knight was a sword master. What was more, he prevented the Monster Domino phenomenon from destroying the kingdom’s eastern area, accommodating tens and thousands of refugees and giving them new homes. This accomplishment designated his fiefdom as the hub for reconstruction in the east by the queen and elevated his family to the stature of a county. What about now? He was no longer the eldest son of a count. He was now appointed as a meritorious retainer, the first one in 130 years.

I never thought I’d witness this in my lifetime…

The hearts of nobles sincerely loyal to the queen raced in excitement. They internalized their emotions as they clenched their fists. They truly admired and congratulated Lloyd for his honor as a meritorious retainer. However, some of the power-grubbing nobles reacted differently.

How dare that country bumpkin…

They stared at Lloyd coldly but couldn’t let their emotions show as the queen made the following declaration.

“Moreover, I proclaim thenceforth that I shall dispatch my loyal Bone Dragon to my faithful servant, Loyd Frontera. The Bone Dragon will reside in Frontera County until I order otherwise. Until then, the Bone Dragon shall support the reconstruction efforts of Frontera county in the east.”

“…”

Now, their lips were sealed in sheer fright. Honor, authority, privilege, and now power? Truly, this was overwhelming and shocking. The nobles that used to look down on Lloyd could not even dare think about showing their envy now. Queen Magentano scoffed when she saw everyone’s reaction. A faint smile appeared on her lips, and she looked at Lloyd.

It’s as I expected. Lloyd Frontera. What a sly and cunning man he is. Everything he said was correct.

Just then, she recalled the conversation that took place 15 days ago when Lloyd visited her office.

“Your Majesty,” suggested Lloyd, “I will lend the Bone Dragon to you. So, you will need to declare at the official event that you will lend the Bone Dragon to me.”

To this, she replied, “Do you mean I should announce I’m lending you the Bone Dragon that you will lend me?”

“Yes, Your Majesty.” Lloyd approached and whispered in her ears with a cunning smile on his face. “I am aware Your Majesty is worried about how you will restore the imperial family’s reputation that was damaged from the previous incident with the Bone Dragon. As such, I believe this method is the best way to solve it.”

“Do you suggest we reveal the Bone Dragon by opening the same banquet?” Queen Megentano shot an incredulous look.

“That is exactly the case, Your Majesty.” Lloyd nodded.

“And assuage those who were left panic-stricken when the Bone Dragon appeared.”

“Yes, Your Majesty,” said Lloyd affirming once more, “That is my exact point. The kingdom’s reputation you are worrying about has been damaged because of the Bone Dragon’s uproar. Therefore, all you have to do is show that the Bone Dragon is loyal to you.”

“Certainly. That sounds promising.” Queen Magentano found herself nodding. Restore her dampened glory by tackling the very source. Nothing could ascertain better results than this. “So, your suggestion to announce that I will be lending the Bone Dragon to you must come from your desire to let it freely roam around people, right?”

“Ahaha… Truly, Your Majesty is the only one who understands me.” Lloyd’s embarrassed smile confirmed one thing for the queen.

Surely, his goal is to live a peaceful life in the province.

At last, Queen Magentano was able to get a vague understanding of what Lloyd was after and why he had come to her.

He came to gain control of the Bone Dragon, but there are currently zero accounts of the Bone Dragon being sighted in the kingdom. In other words, this means that Lloyd had kept the dragon out of everyone’s eyes, probably because he wished to avoid creating suspicion or a scandal.

The Bone Dragon had ransacked the palace and disappeared into thin air. What would happen if the news spreads that the eldest son of the Frontera family masters the Bone Dragon? It’d bring about a wild scandal.

He has been considerate of me, and he plans on remaining unchanged. Up to this point, even now, and in the future as well.

Queen Magentano saw Lloyd in a new light. Right now, Lloyd was advising her to advertise to the world that the Bone Dragon, under Lloyd’s lead, was loyal to her.

Moreover, if I make the declaration that I am lending the Bone Dragon to the Frontera family… Yes. He would be able to have the Bone Dragon around without any hassle while saving my and the royal family’s tainted reputation at the same time.

The solution was beneficial for both of them. Lloyd could put the Bone Dragon to use without being conscious of the prickling stares of people, and she would restore her authority as the queen. Aside from this, there were other potential benefits.

“Hmm, good,” accepted Queen Magentano, smiling at the idea. “I shall take your suggestion. Since everyone outside and inside the kingdom will be more careful around me if things develop as you say.”

“Yes, Your Majesty, that shall be the case,” said Lloyd as he moved back.

“Right. Our reputation suffered horrific damage during the Bone Dragon’s uproar. In the meantime, I’m sure everyone was laughing at us. It must be the case even now. However, if I reveal the Bone Dragon’s loyalty toward us at the banquet, which will happen in fifteen days… Ha. Everyone will think twice about the meaning behind my silence and patience.”

“Yes, Your Majesty,” acknowledged Lloyd. “They will realize that your composed response was a silence filled with strength, the time used to compose a hit list.”

“Yes, indeed.”

It will indeed be the case. She naturally thought so. Even though the actual events would differ from what they advertise, the snickering nobles and diplomats would regard the Magentano House’s silence as intentional. They will think that despite slaying the dragon, the queen had purposefully remained silent and endured all the mockery to assess everyone.

“If events develop that way,” she continued, “No potential enemies inside and outside my kingdom will regard me as an easy target after this.”

“It shall be the case, Your Majesty. Even if an unfavorable or unfortunate incident ever occurs to the imperial family in the future, everyone will have to think twice about their reaction after seeing what will happen.”

“Truly, this event will set an example of our kingdom.”

The House of Magentano would not be looked down on by anyone, and that image was set to bring in a myriad of benefits, including power and political dominance.

Truly, what he said was exactly right.

Jostling out of the conversation with Lloyd in her trance, Queen Magentano gazed through the banquet hall. The frightened and shocked expressions on the faces of top diplomats and nobles were evident. She was satisfied. Their reactions didn’t betray her expectations. In particular, the sight of Lloyd’s astonishment, which was a rare sight, satisfied her the most.

You requested me to make an announcement about the Bone Dragon. But I have other thoughts. The Bone Dragon was yours to begin with. And yet, you want me to pretend otherwise? That makes me feel like I am your puppet.

As such, she presented Lloyd with a special gift that wasn’t mentioned in the deal, adding one more declaration about Lloyd’s elevation of stature as the meritorious retainer. And now, Lloyd was throwing a goggle-eyed stare at the queen, who held his gaze with a smile in her eyes. Lloyd could only smile back.

Wow. She got me good this time.

Lloyd once again felt that the queen was an extraordinary person. She wasn’t someone to mess with. No, anyone who was careless in dealing with her would face dire consequences. A bitter smile appeared on Lloyd’s face.

Woah. I’ve become a meritorious retainer. Is this for real?

He had never made such a request nor expected it. Thanks to the surprise, he understood something.

The queen is trying to secure me as her political ally.

Lloyd could feel her intentions with every fiber of his being.

She must think my powers surpassed hers. So, instead of keeping me in check, she chose to recruit me to her side.

This decision from the queen couldn’t have been an easy one. Normally, kings and queens sought out chances to drag down or kill any powerful individuals under them. But Queen Magentano approached the matter differently by bringing Lloyd onto her side. Her intentions came off even more clearly when he learned of the great privileges granted to meritorious retainers, particularly the right of legal immunity.

The nobles can’t hold me in check anymore and only the queen can bestow a lawful judgment on me. This means that she’ll bear the political liability and condemnation in the case my family does something wrong. It also means that she won’t be willing to let go of me without a fight. Ha. She’s a smart queen.

Part of him wondered if the title “Magentano Carry” had a large influence on the queen’s decision this time.

Anyway, I’ve gotten everything I can from the situation.

Whether he became a meritorious retainer or not, his goal remained unchanged.

Once I stop the restoration of destiny, I’ll live the rest of my life in my fiefdom in peace.

Lloyd remembered his life plan once again. He made a steadfast resolution: From now on, he shall not be baited by the queen’s sweet talks. His life would be that of a rich, unemployed landlord. He carved this resolution into his bones, heart, intestines, and soul. And when the following day came, Lloyd kickstarted his project to lift up the palace from the ground and find the tauranga there.
