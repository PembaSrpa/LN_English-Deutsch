# Smack the Back of the Head and Run (3)

---

“Do you agree?!”

“What?” The queen’s eyebrow trembled. She doubted if she heard him right.

Bestow all of the dragon bones to him? And not enough with that, exempt him from ten years of labor?

Queen Magentano was dumbstruck and couldn’t believe what she just heard. But there wasn’t any time to argue. She quickly scanned her eyes behind herself.

Chomp! About 10 feet behind her, the Bone Dragon aggressively chomped down its jaw once again. If things went wrong, they would be no different from a beetle that had been crushed by a door hinge. There wasn’t any time to hesitate or think.

Lloyd Frontera, you are using this opportunity to be granted an exorbitant request. But…

Giving everything he wanted was better than getting killed here. The queen made a flash judgment and gave her decision without a second thought.

“All right. I approve,” she spoke aloud.

“Really, Your Majesty?!”

“Indeed.”

“Then do you mean that you shall exempt me from labor for the next ten years and give me all of the dragon bones?” asked Lloyd again.

“Indeed.”

“Duly noted. As a neutral party here, Javier Asrahan will stand as the witness to your words! Do you agree, Your Majesty?”

“I said, I do!” huffed the queen in annoyance.

“I am overwhelmed with gratitude, Your Majesty.”

Chomp! Once again, the Bone Dragon bit down his jaw in a close distance.

“Then! Please listen carefully to my plan!” Lloyd pointed to the back of the Bone Dragon’s body. “Look over there! You will see that the 19th tailbone is slightly different from the other surrounding parts!”

“The 19th tailbone?”

“Yes, Your Majesty!”

Queen Magentano and Javier’s gaze traveled to the 19th tailbone. But they failed to notice anything different.

“Strange,” the queen remarked. “To me, they all look the same in both shape and material.”

“No, Your Majesty!” Lloyd answered with conviction before making a big confession. “In actuality, Your Majesty! That is not a piece of bone but granite!”

“What…? Granite?”

“Yes, Your Majesty! Do you recall how Your Majesty promised to bestow me a chunk of the dragon’s tailbone?”

“Yes, I do!” answered the queen. “However, did you not report to me that you planned on using the spine of an emperor whale, not granite? You said it was an excellent replacement due to its impressive durability and quality.”

“Your Majesty is correct!”

“But what happened?” Queen Magentano wondered why the fake bone was made of granite, not the backbone of the emperor whale.

“The backbone of the emperor whale was too expensive!” Lloyd yelled another confession.

“…”

“As such, I argued to use granite instead and pushed forward with it!”

“…“

"When we cut and refined the granite, it was exactly the same. It would be difficult to distinguish between the dragon’s tailbone and the granite with the naked eye! Right now, even Your Majesty could not tell the difference between the two! What was more, I thought it was a better option since the granite was one-tenth of the cost of the emperor whale’s backbone!”

“You wouldn’t have dared to embezzle the leftover money, am I right?” shot back the queen at Lloyd.

“Not at all, Your Majesty!”

Lloyd quickly shook his head. He really didn’t pocket the leftover money and simply reduced the price of the construction.

“The dragon skeleton was only meant to be admired from a distance! I judged that it is more efficient to use granite since it looks the same to the naked eye and holds enough durability to support the overall skeleton structure! I believed that the money saved here could be used for a better purpose in the future!”

Lloyd was genuine about this. Though the palace’s finances weren’t his business, he hated wasting money on a construction project. If there was another alternative with the same function, safety, and aesthetics as his initial choice in material, and was a mere one percent of its cost, Lloyd figured that it was more economical to use the cheaper option. That was why he insisted on using the granite and went on with it.

“That is why, Your Majesty! The decision I made back then! It will become the key to destroying this dragon!”

Chomp! About six feet behind, the Bone Dragon chomped down its jaw once again. While their conversation was taking place, Ggoming relentlessly flew up and down. Like a fly desperately trying to lose a giant eagle, Ggoming flew until his wings were drenched in sweat.

“I personally think,” Lloyd went on to shout, “that the 19th prosthetic tailbone is receiving the effects of the mana power that maintains the dragon’s body!”

“I agree. That must explain how it can move with such coordination with the other joints,” seconded the queen.

“Yes, Your Majesty. It must also mean that it will be much harder than most other pieces of granite.”

“And yet, it will be weaker compared to the other bones,” said the queen, understanding his point.

“That truly is the case, Your Majesty!” beamed Lloyd. “What is more! If that bone is shattered, the whole tail will snap off and the dragon won’t be able to fly anymore like now!”

What Lloyd said was obvious. The tail wasn’t a decorative one. It was an indispensable organ, more so when it came to flight.

It allows the dragon to maintain balance!

But once the tail was lost, the Bone Dragon would drop after losing balance and wouldn’t be able to fly again, much like an aircraft with a lost tail wing.

Then my original plan to use the dragon king won’t work anymore. So, this is my best option for now. It’s better than getting our heads crushed and becoming one with nature after being caught by the Bone Dragon.

Choose an effective strike instead of immediate death. What comes next can be sketched out later.

Thinking so, Lloyd hurriedly shouted, “Therefore! I will become a bait!“

"You will?” asked the queen.

“Yes, Your Majesty!”

The queen’s eyes widened. Javier’s eyebrow trembled.

Lloyd smiled wryly as he looked at both of them. “Who among us can fly better with Ggoming in harmony but me?”

That was a fact.

“Therefore, I will become its bait and draw its attention. Meanwhile, please crush its 19th tailbone.”

Lloyd put his hand into his pocket and brought out Ppodong and Bangul.

“These kids will be your help.”

“Ppodong!”

“Bangul!”

Lloyd fed the red sunflower seed to Ppodong and Bangul, whose eyes were glimmering. And he threw them toward the ground, each of them growing large with a boom and landing safely on the ground.

“Please get down now!” urged Lloyd.

“…”

Even until then, Queen Magentano stared at Lloyd without saying anything. Lloyd smiled, only a corner of his mouth rising.

“Your Majesty, we do not have much time. Please hurry,” urged Lloyd again.

“Then you-”

“Please do not worry about me.”

“No,” she said as she shook her head. “I am not worried about you. I am simply stupefied that you just revealed your usage of the granite in place of the emperor whale’s backbone just now.”

“…”

“Had this not happened, you would have never said anything, right?” pointed out the queen.

“…”

“Technically speaking, that is obviously an act of embezzlement-”

“I pray you to be safe, Your Majesty!”

Urgent, Lloyd reined to flip Ggoming upside down, and the unexpected turn caused the queen and Javier to dangle from their saddle. They then let go of their grips as there was no time for argument.

Ten years of labor exemption? Do you dare think you can slip out of my hands so easily? It is easy to tie you down. I simply have to ask you to choose between the punishment of embezzling or discarding the labor exemption.

But now, survival and preventing that awful disaster in the capital came first. The wry smile on her face vanished as she landed on the ground. And now, her eyes shone with determination. Javier, who landed beside her, nodded. They had a momentary exchange of eyes. That was enough for these two sword masters.

Sprint! Each ran in the opposite direction as they passed through the shrubs, where a fantastical creature waited in each place.

“Sir Ppodong, nice to meet you.”

“Ppodong!”

Javier got on Ppodong’s plump back, and the latter started to zoom off. On the other end of the waterway, the queen met Bangul.

“Is your name Bangul?” the queen asked.

“Bangul!”

“My memory is right,” she said. “I look forward to fighting with you.”

“Bba-bangul! Bangul!”

The queen got on Bangul’s back. And the latter started to gulp the water in the waterway. And she wagged her tail very earnestly. Jingle! Jingle!

She alerted the surrounding with her melodious warning, and the moment she lifted her tail, a massive explosion burst from her buttock.

Gurgle! Rumble!

Bangul quickly digested the water she drank. A large amount of boiling steam jetted out of her like an explosive volcano. Kaboom!

Steam shot out of her with incredible pressure, and Bangul glided through the waterway like a motor banana boat. The queen lifted her sword on top of her.

Lloyd Frontera.

Her gaze moved upward toward the night sky. Ggoming and Lloyd were frantically flying about in a desperate effort to run away from the Bone Dragon that was madly chasing after them.

I do not wish to lose you.

An excellent subject. A reliable worker. But at the same time, a man she was immensely grateful toward. She didn’t wish to lose Lloyd Frontera in a place like this.

Never.

Clench! The back of her hand that gripped the sword tightened. Meanwhile, Javier was making a similar resolution as the queen.

Master Lloyd.

He was sprinting across the garden riding Ppodong, and his gaze was fixed on Lloyd above.

I had no idea that this was your plan, Master Lloyd.

Javier bit his underlip. Once again, he had another misunderstanding. He was wrong to think that Master Lloyd would be someone who ran away in this kind of situation, a pathetic coward who fled to survive all by himself. Javier realized that he was too rash in judging his young master that way.

This happened in Cremo too.

He remembered how he had harshly expressed his disappointment in Master Lloyd, saying he wasn’t aware of his rotten character.

It’s the same today.

Javier realized that he was the foolish one. Lloyd was the type of man who was always ready to sacrifice himself.

So… I will protect you, so you will not have to sacrifice yourself.

Clench! Javier made a resolve in his heart. He would push aside his own safety if it meant protecting his young master. He wouldn’t stand back even at the risk of his own life.

You must remain safe no matter what, Young Master.

Wishing so, Javier encouraged Ppodong to ride faster. Meanwhile, Lloyd was struggling by himself.

Huff! Queen! Javier! Hurry up a little!

Chomp! The Bone Dragon bit down right behind him. Chills ran down Lloyd’s spine. One wrong move and he was sure to get crushed.

But… this is still doable!

The queen and Javier left, so that was one less thing for him to worry about. Ggoming’s movements became slightly more relaxed.

All right. I just need to keep running away like now!

Thankfully, the Bone Dragon had its eyes on Lloyd and nothing else. It almost felt obsessive. Lloyd wondered if he owed some kind of debt to this dragon.

Argh! So hurry up!

Lloyd wondered how on earth he ended up in this situation, playing tag with the Bone Dragon. But he suppressed the emerging misery inside of him. He sparingly shot out mana blasts, ensuring that the dragon’s attention was focused only on him. He urgently worked in unison with Ggoming as they flew. Their flight consisted of quick turns and sudden drops to create a distraction.

Lloyd flew in the opposite direction of its flight at times, gliding over its back and passing through its ribs. Enraged, the Bone Dragon wielded its foreleg and scratched its ribs, missing Lloyd by just a hair’s breadth. Lloyd then slipped right through its waist. Their altitude lowered with another drop. At the same time, he shook Ggoming’s bag.

Pssh! Multiple bundles of spider webs discharged from his bag, sticking to the dragon’s face. Lloyd used that chance to widen their distance, flying lower and gliding over the treetops in the garden. His ears picked up the booming noise that ensued behind him. It was the Bone Dragon hurtling their way with even more speed.

Fwoosh! It propelled its whole body at a shattering speed as if it was trying to cover the entire night sky.

“…!“

Lloyd sensed it. He couldn’t avoid the dragon this time. The Bone Dragon sensed it too. So, in other words, this was Lloyd’s chance.

"Now!”

Lloyd bawled, his voice sounding like a bolt of lightning. Just then, a volcanic-like explosion burst from the woods below.

Boooom!

There was a cave Ppodong had dug in advance, where Bangul was hiding. Her buttock pointed toward the sky from the cave and produced a volcanic explosion. The heat and ash jetted up in a frenzy and covered the area Lloyd just passed by. This blinded the Bone Dragon’s vision for a second.

…!

The Bone Dragon couldn’t see anything ahead of it. Targa, the soul trapped in the Bone Dragon, was flustered. But his panic was short-lived.

This trick doesn’t work against me!

Targa had the power of the Bone Dragon. He could use its raw strength to penetrate the thick smoke.

Boom! The Bone Dragon’s wings tore across the air, moving faster as it generated shockwaves. At that moment, Targa’s sight and consciousness were glued to the back of Lloyd, faintly visible through the thick smoke. So focused and obsessed he was that he didn’t notice it. He didn’t notice that just outside the thick plume of dark smoke that he was passing through, two sword masters were aiming at its weakness with their sharp blades.

Fwish! Javier launched a mana blast beyond the thick layer of smoke.

Fwoosh! The mana blast split through the air and flew at a supersonic speed to hit the Bone Dragon’s 19th tailbone. Bullseye. It penetrated the bone, creating a 20-inch hole in a split second. Cracks formed around the hole. The Bone Dragon’s whole body shook.

“…!“

Targa panicked. Just then, another sword master immediately launched an attack.

"Ppodong!”

Ppodong, in an impressive show of strength, hurled the queen like a ball, and she was catapulted into the air. Her sword was wrapped in a giant aura.

Slash! Her aura sword pierced the 19th tailbone so perfectly. This tailbone had been struck with the mana blast already and was a fake, granite one at the brilliant decision of Lloyd’s cost-cutting scheme. So, it wasn’t able to hold any longer even if it was protected by the body-maintenance magic of the dragon.

Crack! The 19th tailbone explosively splintered into pieces, and right after that, the dragon’s whole tail fell off its body.

Argh!

Targa yelled in dismay. The Bone Dragon, which was hurtling like a wild boar, lost its balance, and it started to flail and fumble in the air like a crab. And thus, an unstoppable descent began.
