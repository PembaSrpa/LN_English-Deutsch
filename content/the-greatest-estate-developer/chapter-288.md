# The Second Key Construction Material (1)

---

“Eh…?”

Ice Dragon Tyrannus narrowed his eyes in his cave. The cave sat on the rough land beyond the Arctic Sea, south to the Lorasian Continent.

Just what was that?

Tyrannus could feel that the Heart of Winter had vanished. Though it happened in an ever-so-distant land and the item was old, the dragon felt it nonetheless.

A human absorbed it. But… How is that possible?

Tyrannus cocked his head as he recalled the day he gave the Heart of Winter to a human as a gift. It must have been a few hundred years ago. Mikael Magentano, his friend, paved the path of a new era after being touted as the hero among humans. And just as a good friend would do, Tyrannus presented him with a gift. A marble that was infused with his mana and contained the frigid air of winter.

I remember being extremely excited at that time. I got so preoccupied that I produced an artifact more powerful than I originally designed it to be. So powerful that it even possessed a faint consciousness of its own.

Tyrannus had to suffer from a cold that whole year after manufacturing the artifact. But thanks to its devotion and sacrifice, Tyrannus formed a spiritual connection with the Heart of Winter, which was the reason why he could hear the items’ whispers carried by the wind a few days ago.

“The Heart is free.” With that, it was absorbed into a human. This flustered Tyrannus.

Out of all the many races, it had to assimilate into a human’s manaheart. I didn’t know that was even possible.

Tyrannus became curious. It considered flying over where it happened at once, the impulse to satisfy his curiosity surging in him. But he eventually shook his head.

No. I can’t ruin my precious time off like this.

Tyrannus objectively analyzed his situation. He was enjoying a restful break after his wife went on a trip with his daughter-in-law. It was a break that had come for the first time in hundreds of years, so a slightly surprising or intriguing incident wasn’t going to make him ruin his precious vacation.

Tsk. Who cares. It’s not something that’s meant to be absorbed. He’s going to have an awful stomachache. I guess they did what they had to do. Live or die, it’s their business, not mine. Boom!

Boom! Tyrannus resigned and let its head fall flat on the ground, and soon, the soft snoring filled the cave.

♣️

At the same hour in Capital Magentano, astonishment filled Lloyd’s eyes.

Dang.

Half flustered and satisfied, he hurriedly scanned the message before his eyes like a starving man who was unexpectedly gifted a bowl of ramen in the middle of the night.

It has been a while since I received an accomplishment notification. And the reward is what? 2,000 RP? This is nuts!

Lloyd didn’t believe it at first. He wondered if he mistakenly counted an extra zero. He rubbed his eyes and read it again. Still, it read 2,000 RP. Now, he had 4,655 RP.

Haha. Hahaha.

The corners of his lips crawled up on its own. Lloyd instantly knew what he could do with this reward.

I can now use the Time Freeze skill anytime I want to.

Lloyd now held the power to freeze time anywhere except for a zone of his designation. As long as he was in that area, he could carry on with his activities for a year without getting older. Though it was just a year, it was enough time to finish construction of any size.

That’s not it. The achievement message explained that the mere excavation of the key material for the jewel’s construction is highly impressive and will be remembered throughout history.

That was how he was able to acquire 2,000 RP in one go. And this meant…

From now on, I can receive somewhat similar RP every time I find a key material for this construction.

2,000 RP for every key material found. 3,000 RP for the Time Freeze skill.

So, let’s say that I spend 3,000 RP and freeze time. While the world stands still, I can go on and excavate another key material for construction. That’d give me 2,000 RP again. Wow. I feel like I’ve earned some sort of cash refund.

Of course, there was a big difference of 1,000 RP for both.

But it’s all right. 1,000 RP is a manageable amount.

Sure, it was burdensome, but it wasn’t impossible. And because it wasn’t impossible, he felt encouraged to push ahead with his future plans.

I can save time by using the Time Freeze skill for all future excavations. That will make it easier for me to stop the restoration of destiny.

The more he calculated and made estimations for the future, the happier his humming became. However, he didn’t get lost in the rose-colored future.

“Okay, don’t loosen up just yet! This is the dangerous part, so pull yourselves together! Push! Two, three!”

“Ppodong!”

“Argh!!!”

Crush! Lloyd’s energetic shout filled the cave, and at his yell, Ppodong and the laborers exerted their efforts, pushing and pulling the piece of tauranga until it was brought outside. As for getting it out to the ground from the vertical cave, that job fell in the mighty hands of Bibeong.

“Okay! Lift it up!”

“Bibeong!”

Despite how the pieces of tauranga were sixteen ft in width and nineteen ft in length, they were just tiny stones to Bibeong. And so, the twelve pieces of the tauranga were soon brought out to the ground under the sun for the first time after hundreds of years. There was more work for Lloyd that followed after, however.

Of course. Since I bored, dug, and opened the area beneath the palace, it’s time to fill it back up.

In the first place, this construction involved boring a giant tunnel under the palace’s foundation.

If I leave the tunnel untouched, it will collapse over time until the whole foundation sinks.

Then, the palace would also collapse as a result since it sat on top of the foundation. Since that was the last thing Lloyd wanted, he shoved rocks and cement into every crevice of the tunnel and refrigerant room, making sure that they were completely filled. And finally, after ten more days, everything was over. The time has come to leave the capital.

Queen Magentano looked at Lloyd. “You are here to greet me farewell before you leave, am I right?”

“That is correct, Your Majesty,” answered Lloyd, bowing his head down.

A wry smile appeared on her face. “Then have you achieved what you came here for?”

“That is also right, Your Majesty,” answered Lloyd.

“Are you not overwhelmed with gratitude?”

“I was just about to add that, Your Majesty.”

“I wouldn’t have hated you at least if you couldn’t talk. And I suppose now is when you’ll ask for my forgiveness.”

Lloyd flinched. “You know me well, Your Majesty…”

“Certainly. Moreover, there is something else.”

“Something else that I need your forgiveness for, Your Majesty?”

“Of course.”

For some reason, he felt that her voice sounded accusatory. “What is that…?”

“Do you still not know?”

Lloyd didn’t know what to say. Here she was suddenly cornering him with her words, but he couldn’t remember what more fault he made. He merely looked up at the queen, who began to click her tongue, which probably was because of his curious gaze.

“Tsk, tsk. A man who only knows about himself and always comes full of empty promises. What a lamentable man you are.”

“Promise, Your Majesty?” Promise? What promise? Lloyd thought to himself as he cocked his head to the side. The wry smile on her face deepened.

“It appears that you have forgotten about your brazen words to me,” reminded the queen. “Do you not remember the conversation we had before the start of the construction and when you came to request for the Heart of Winter? Do you still not recall what I asked and what you replied?”

“Um… Oh. Right…” It all came back to Lloyd.

“I asked you if you could promise me that your project that involved the usage of the Heart of Winter and harvesting tauranga would not harm my imperial family or me. Do you remember what you said?”

“I said, ‘Certainly, Your Majesty…”

“I am glad that you have not forgotten your words.”

Lloyd’s face turned red in embarrassment.

“At that time, I naively trusted you with my heart and accepted to grant your request. It was your promise, and I chose to trust you. No other words were necessary. But what happened to that promise?”

Lloyd didn’t say anything but looked down in shame.

“Hmm, how should I get compensated for the destroyed item? What compensation should I demand from a shameless man like yourself.”

“Um, hmm, I profusely ask for your forgive-”

“You are not trying to amend your wrongdoing with mere words, aren’t you?”

“Um, hmm, I truly do not know-”

“You cannot be trying to move on by saying you don’t know how to gain my forgiveness, right?”

“T-then, what is it that Your Majesty wants from-”

“You cannot be asking me to spell that out for you either, right?”

“….”

Please save me, my queen. Or just beat me, scold me, or kill me. Lloyd looked up at the queen with a sullen, gloomy face. This softened her smile.

“It is done. I already declared at the court that I shall not penalize you. So, what need is there to rebuke you now?”

“I am aware, but still…”

“Still?”

Lloyd’s resolute eyes shone as they fixed on her. “I will absolutely return this favor one day, Your Majesty.”

“Favor?”

“Yes, Your Majesty.”

Lloyd hung his head down. This time, he was serious. It wasn’t a joke.

She has been taking great care of me.

In hindsight, it was true. She was always there to give help or assistance during critical moments. This time, too, her help was so priceless that it couldn’t be measured in terms of money. One thing led to another, and he even absorbed the divine item that belonged to the imperial family.

Any ordinary ruler would have seen red. No, they might have been losing sleep trying to think of a way to destroy and kill me on the very day I came to possess powers greater than them.

But Queen Magentano was different. She instead made him a political comrade with a big heart. And even now, she was showering him with generous assistance and help and supporting his dream of being a rich bum. Not only that, but she also nonchalantly let the incident with the Heart of Winter slide. No one could have a bigger heart than her.

“Therefore, Your Majesty, when you find yourself in trouble, I shall be by your side to overcome it together with you.”

Lloyd spoke sincerely. What he said was against his go-to motto, “Remember what they did for you for a second. Remember what they did to you for life.” But could it be that she wasn’t happy with it? She scoffed.

“Hmm, only once?”

“Your Majesty?!” asked Lloyd in surprise.

“Never mind. Well, once, I see. I should be grateful. This single act of gratitude can’t be easy to obtain from a man who only visits when he wants something from me.”

“…”

“Based on your behaviors to this date, I suppose I should be tearing up in gratitude if you keep it in the future.”

Lloyd gaped in shock.

“So, first, I will only receive your gesture. You can leave now.”

“I am overwhelmed with gratitude, Your Majesty,” replied Lloyd.

“Again. There,” she said as she shook her head. “The gratitude you say only when you are stuck.”

“Um, then, what should I say instead, Your Majesty?”

“Instead of showing me your gratitude, show me your sincerity.”

“Sincerity, Your Majesty?”

“Yes.”

“Then how should I do-”

“That is for you to think and find out. Did you not hear my order that you can leave now?”

“Not at all, Your Majesty. I shall leave now.”

“Tsk. This is the only time you pretend to listen.”

“Your Majesty?”

“I was talking to myself. Do not be curious. So long.”

“Yes, Your Majesty. Long live the queen.”

At last… He was free! Lloyd inwardly yelled in joy as he bolted out of her presence at lightning speed. He swooshed out of the palace and got on the back of Draggy, who was already on standby. Javier, Ppodong, Bangul, and Ggoming were already there.

“So, Bibeong, go back to the Frontera fiefdom. And say bye to Bangul.”

“Bi, Bibeong! Bibeong!”

“Bangul! Bba-bangul!”

Bibeong was, unfortunately, too large to be carried around like the rest of his friends. Moreover, since the Termina Garden was well-repaired, he had no reason to stay in the capital. Bibeong and Bangul parted wistfully as they hugged. And soon, Draggy flew up, its hands tightly clenching the twelve pieces of tauranga.

Fwoosh! Crash! Once it soared up, it turned northward, its fierce fluttering leaving a shockwave. At almost a supersonic speed, they headed north. After several hours of flight, they arrived at the Arctic Sea.

There, Lloyd ordered, “Drop!”

Clack! Splash! Draggy dropped the Taurangas from 10,000 feet above. They sank into the sea. Lloyd waited until they hit the water and plunged down to the foundation where the Jewel of Truth would be built. Soon, a welcoming message emerged.

Ding dong.

[You have dropped the twelve pieces of tauranga safely near the foundation of the Jewel of Truth.]

[For this, you now can access information on the Taupo, the second key material for the Jewel of Truth.]

[Would you like to access information on the Taupo now?]

[YES / NO]

Of course, it’s a yes.

Lloyd silently nodded. Just then…

Fwoosh! A new video emerged and clouded his vision.
